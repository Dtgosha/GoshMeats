Our Story

Harare's Freshest Butchery, At Your Doorstep

Gosha Meats was born from a simple belief: every family in Harare deserves access to fresh, quality meat without the trip to the market. We started small — a cold room, a sharp knife, and a bicycle — and we've grown by earning the trust of our customers, one delivery at a time.

Every cut is hand-selected and prepared fresh daily. No frozen stock, no day-old product. When you order before midday, you can expect delivery the same afternoon. Our riders know Harare's neighbourhoods inside-out, so whether you're in Avondale, Borrowdale, Mbare or Mabelreign — we'll find you.

We're a local business, serving local families. That's never going to change.
